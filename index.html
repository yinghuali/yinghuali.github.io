Biography

I am a Doctoral Researcher at University of Luxembourg, supervised by Prof. Tegawendé F. Bissyandé (ERC Fellow), Prof. Jacques Klein and Prof. Lei Ma. Before that, I received my Master degree from Chongqing University, under the supervision of Prof. Jun Zeng. I have worked as a machine learning engineer in the industry for several years.
