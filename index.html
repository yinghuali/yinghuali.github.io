Biography

I am a Research Associate at the University of Luxembourg, working with Prof. Tegawendé F. Bissyandé (ERC Fellow) and Prof. Jacques Klein. I completed my PhD degree at the University of Luxembourg under the supervision of Prof. Tegawendé F. Bissyandé (ERC Fellow), Prof. Jacques Klein and Prof. Lei Ma. Before that, I received my Master's degree from Chongqing University, under the supervision of Prof. Jun Zeng. I have worked as a machine learning engineer in the industry for several years.
